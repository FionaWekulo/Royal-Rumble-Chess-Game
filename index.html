about us

Royal Rumble is a person versus person chess game.

Welcome to our PvP chess game, where strategy meets competition in the ultimate test of skill and intellect. Our team of game developers was inspired to create this PvP chess game after a personal experience with the traditional game of chess.

Having loved playing chess and appreciated the mental stimulation it provided, we often found ourselves wishing for more excitement and competitiveness during the game.

After some brainstorming, we realized that the addition of player vs player (PvP) gameplay could be the perfect solution.

By pitting players against each other in a chess match, we could add an element of rivalry and excitement to the game, without detracting from the strategy and mental acuity required to win.
